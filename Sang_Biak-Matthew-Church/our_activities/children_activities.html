Armored for God

We will be stuffing boxes on July 15h at 4:30p.m. for Armored for God as part of our Mission for VBS. We supported an organization known as KISS this year, which is Kids in Support of Soldiers. This is an organization that sends packages to soldiers who are stationed overseas. We are still collecting items and would appreciate your help. We need lip balm, slim jims, pudding or fruit cups, individual drink mixes for water bottles and hard candy. If you are able to bring in any of these items, it would be greatly appreciated. If you know of a friend or relative who is currently serving overseas, please provide us with the APO address and we will be sure that they receive a package each month. Thank you for your help!!
