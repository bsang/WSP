Yarn Works

For those of you who may not be familiar with YARN WORKS, the purpose of the ministry is to create prayer shawls and blankets as an outward and visible sign of the mystical reality of prayer that knits our souls together in Christ. These garments are given to the sick, homebound, poor, and elderly in our midst to comfortably remind them that they are present to us and bound to us in community even in their loneliness, illness, pain, and exhaustion.

We will hold meetings on the 2nd and 4th Sunday of each month from 5 p.m. to 6 p.m. in the parish hall.

You don't have to know how to knit. There are several experienced knitters who will be happy to teach you. It's not difficult and we'll have fun while we work together.

To knit a prayer shawl you will need one pair of number 13 needles and 3 balls of homespun yarn.
